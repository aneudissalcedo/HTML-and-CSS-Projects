The Novice is the starting class for all human characters in Ragnarok Online.
Later you can choose to become a swordsman, mage, archer, merchant, thief, or acolyte.

Click to Enlarge

Swordmen are honest and as stubborn as the strong waves of the ocean.
With strong physical attacks, defense, and a sense of command,
a Swordman can become a headstrong leader to others.

Click to Enlarge

Mages rely on their skills as their main source of damage, and such attacks
are magically based and only take into account the target's Magical Defense,
and not their Physical Defense.

Click to Enlarge

Archers are experts at long-range combat with Bows and Arrows.
Their range gives them several advantages, such as the ability to
change the element of their weapon quickly by changing their arrow type.

Always on the lookout for profit, Merchants are the economic experts of Ragnarok Online.
Setting out to make a fortune, playing as a Merchant will allow the player
to get the most out of every last zeny.

Waiting in the shadows, planning where they'll strike next, the mischievous Thieves
are a force to reckon with. Swift and powerful, few can keep up with a Thief's attack speed and power,
whether they're slashing their way in battle with a sharp dagger, or striking from afar with a bow.

Acolytes live their lives serving the Catholic Church and following the religious teachings.
They train to become selfless and devoted individuals, aiming to help all that they meet.
