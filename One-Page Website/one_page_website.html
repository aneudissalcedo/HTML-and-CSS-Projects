Ragnarok Online Classes

Classes

Novice

The Novice is the starting class for all human characters in
Ragnarok Online. Later you can choose to become a
swordsman, mage, archer, merchant, thief, or acolyte.

Swordman

Swordmen are honest and as stubborn as the strong waves of the
ocean. With strong physical attacks, defense, and a sense of
command, a Swordman can become a headstrong leader to others.

Mage

Mages rely on their skills as their main source of damage, and such
attacks are magically based and only take into account the target's
Magical Defense, and not their Physical Defense.

Archer

Archers are experts at long-range combat with Bows and Arrows.
Their range gives them several advantages, such as the ability to
change the element of their their arrow type quickly.

Merchant

Always on the lookout for profit, Merchants are the economic experts
of Ragnarok Online. Setting out to make a fortune, playing as
a Merchant will allow the player to get the most out of every last zeny.

Thief

Waiting in the shadows, planning where they'll strike next,
the mischievous Thieves are a force to reckon with. Swift
and powerful, few can keep up with a Thief's attack speed and power.

Acolyte

Acolytes live their lives serving the Catholic Church and following
the religious teachings. They train to become selfless
and devoted individuals, aiming to help all that they meet.
